# Monetization Playbook - lakehouse-contract-lab

This file is a commercial planning artifact, not a guarantee of revenue. Use it to make the repository legible as a sellable product, pilot, workshop, license, or proof-of-value package.

## Commercial Lane

- **Lane:** B2B data quality and migration
- **Primary buyer:** Data platform, BI, analytics engineering, and warehouse migration teams.
- **Revenue wedge:** Contract-first medallion pipeline with quality gates and rejected-row evidence.

## Offer Ladder

1. **Data-contract starter pack** - pricing hypothesis: $5k-$12k assessment
2. **Quality-gate implementation** - pricing hypothesis: $20k-$70k implementation
3. **Warehouse migration readiness review** - pricing hypothesis: $3k-$12k/month data quality ops

## Acquisition Channels

- Data engineering articles
- Contract failure demo
- Migration-readiness checklist

## Proof Gates Before Selling

- make verify passes
- CI passes without Spark hang
- Quality reports are inspectable

## Risk Boundaries

- Fixture data only
- Production lineage required
- Warehouse exports need scoped credentials

## 30/60/90 Day GTM

- **30 days:** Publish contract failure examples
- **60 days:** Create dbt/Spark comparison guide
- **90 days:** Offer quality-gate sprint

## Metrics That Matter

- Rejected-row visibility
- Contract coverage
- Pipeline failure lead time

## Positioning Note

Lead with the specific operational pain, then show the runnable proof, then offer a narrow pilot. Do not pitch the repository as a vague app; pitch it as a concrete workflow with evidence, risk boundaries, and a next buying step.
